# US-12.3 - Market Operations & Config Governance (Dual-Auth)

**Epic:** Admin & Audit Controls  
**Phase:** Phase 12 - Admin & Audit  
**Status:** Defined (Refined)  
**Priority:** High  
**Complexity:** Medium  

## User Story

As a **Platform Administrator**,  
I want to control the operational parameters of the platform and the market state,  
so that I can manage trading sessions with **Dual-Approval** safety and **Break-Glass** emergency overrides.

## Acceptance Criteria

### 1. Market State Overrides
- [ ] **Safety Matrix**: Admins cannot override a `HALT` triggered by the Risk Engine without **Dual Approval** and **Step-Up MFA**.
- [ ] **Maintenance Mode**: Enable a gated state where only specific test accounts can trade; triggers automated logout for all other users.

### 2. Configuration & Fee Governance
- [ ] **Non-Retroactive Fees**: Fee changes must include an `effective_at` timestamp in the future; historical trades remain untouched.
- [ ] **Versioning & Rollback**: All system configs (margins, limits, fees) must be versioned with a 1-click rollback capability (requires Checker approval).

### 3. Reliability & Validation
- [ ] **Input Constraints**: Fee percentages and margin multipliers must have hard-coded boundaries (e.g., Fees 0.01% - 1.0%).
- [ ] **Holiday Integrity**: Prevent duplicate or invalid dates in the holiday calendar.

## Business Analyst Insights
> [!WARNING]
> Configuration errors can cause catastrophic market events. Dual-approval for state changes ensures that "fat-finger" errors are caught by a second pair of eyes before they impact the live market.
